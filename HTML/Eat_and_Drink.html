Kaiseki Matsuri

Experience the essence of Japanese cuisine at Kaiseki Matsuri. Every ingredient is imported directly from Japan, giving you an authentic taste of Kaiseki. For every Kaiseki meal purchased, diners will receive a complimentary glass of Sake.

Hours

  • Lunch 12 - 2:30pm (Daily)
  • Dinner 6 - 10pm (Daily)

Ristorante Bello Sapore

Embrace the authentic flavors of Italy at Ristorante Bello Sapore. From handmade pasta to wood-fired pizzas and traditional desserts, there are many Italian specialties available for diners. Complement your meal with a wine from our handpicked Italian selection.

Hours

  • Lunch 12pm - 2:30pm (Daily)
  • Dinner 6pm - 10pm (Daily)

The Epicurean Table

Welcome to The Epicurean Table, a beacon of luxury dining. Our restaurant embodies elegance, offering a captivating setting to enjoy our superb Western cuisine. Our chefs, masters of their craft, use only the finest ingredients, ensuring each dish delivers a gourmet experience.

Hours

  • Lunch 12 - 3pm (Daily)
  • Dinner 5pm - 10:30pm (Daily)
  • Bar 5 - 10:30 (Mon-Sat) ; 5 - 9pm (Sun)
